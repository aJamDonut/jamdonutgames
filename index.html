License to Clone

License to Clone is our latest mad-scientist-esque creation, a wild west sci-fi RPG taking place on a desert planet.

Find and explore our huge open world with no loading screens or cuts, just pure crazy fiction in a world with no rules... Don't like that questgiver? Turn him into food... Do you like that dog over there? Make it yours! Better yet, now you even have food for the dog!
